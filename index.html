Goals and Objectives

Our mision as an organization is to utilize our members educational backgrounds to be able to focus on our core values: Design, Flying, and Competitions.

Design

Many of our members with an engineering background are very interested in designing and builiding our own drones. Our club president has pioneered this and finished our club first drone in Spring 2017.

Flying

The primary focus of this club is to fly drones, and that's what we all love to do.

Compete

Specifically, we're a drone racing club. Our overall goal is to create an intercollegiate drone racing league with an annual national competition to showcase our skills against other universities.
